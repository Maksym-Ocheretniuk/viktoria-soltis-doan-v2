The Dragon and The Drandma

Cute, relatable characters and an irresistibly cool dragon. What more could a kid ask for?

In "The Dragon and the Grandma" follow the heartwarming journey of Grandma Astrid as she discovers the true meaning of love, care, and acceptance in this enchanting tale.

With whimsical and funny moments, "The Dragon and the Grandma" will delight young readers and captivate their imagination. Astrid's adventures with her peculiar pet challenge her at first, but through a series of events, she discovers the power of pure love and compassion. As their bond deepens, Astrid learns to embrace the Dragon's unique qualities, realizing that appearances do not define the worth of a friendship.
