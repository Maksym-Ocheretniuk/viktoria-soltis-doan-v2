Apple Pete and his Little Trouble

Do you have any friends that underestimate you, say that you can't handle something? Or maybe, such thoughts appear in your mind. Which are like small worm “gnaws from the inside”.

This is a funny and a bit philosophical book to help you to understand how important it is to be yourself and believe in your strength. It is very important to trust yourself. Also, it is essential to understand who is next to you and give you some advice. Is this a friend or an enemy or a so-called frenemy?

This story is about a trustful apple Pete, who has a “friend” Worm. His bad advice constantly prevents Apple's decisions. Or, perhaps, these are the thoughts of Apple himself? “I can't”, “But, what if ...?”, “I don't have enough strength”, “It is too hard”. And although there are many obstacles on Apple’s path, he overcomes them successfully. And the most important thing is that he finally understands where all his little troubles come from. After all, everything could go much easier from the very beginning. Now he understands! His mind is pure from doubts and bad thoughts. He finally finds a friend and true inner freedom. He quits worrying and starts to act.

Apple Pete aspires to teach children the importance of being self confident.It encourages positive behavior, cognitive thinking, a growth mindset, empathy and positivity.
